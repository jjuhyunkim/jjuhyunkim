      <h2>Research Summary</h2>
      <p>
        I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Adam Phillippy group at NIH NHGRI, working on complete genome assembly,
        genome curation, and haplotype-resolved analysis. My work focuses on telomere-to-telomere assembly quality,
        epigenomic interpretation, and methods development for difficult genomic regions.
      </p>
      <p>
        I developed Verkko-Fillet for assembly curation and contributed to complete assemblies across human, cell line,
        and giraffe projects. I am also developing methods for parent-of-origin haplotype inference using imprinted
        methylation signatures and tools such as DJCounter for acrocentric digital junction analysis.
      </p>
    </section>
